How it works

Sign up for your free account.

It takes just a couple of minutes to register, no payment required. Get started today and experience the 3D-printing revolution.

Verify your email address.

We’ll send you an email to confirm we have the right email for you. Just click the link and you’ll be on your way.

We’ll invite you to our FormLabs group.

You need a FormLabs printer and account to use Braces On Demand. FormLabs provides the interface to connect our designs to their printers. Here’s how it works.

  • We create a group for your practice and Braces On Demand to share.
  • Once your group is created, we send you an invitation.
  • Don’t worry: your new group is additive. It won’t take away or change anything else you use FormLabs for.

Accept our FormLabs group invitation.

You’ll confirm by logging into FormLabs that you are authorized to join the Braces On Demand group on behalf of your practice.

Build your first print order.

We’ve designed our user interface to be easy to use and intuitive. Print individual patient kits, 10-packs of brackets or tubes for a given tooth position, or select a free to print item. It’s fun to experiment with the more than 8 million available device variants.

Make sure you’ve completed your account setup.

Before printing your first order containing a medical device, make sure you’ve finalized setting up your account. You need a payment method on file and verification of your medical license.

Select Printer & Checkout.

To print your first order of braces, click the checkout button in your shopping cart, verify the printer and resin you want to use, and click to print. Your order will be sent to your FormLabs printer and begin printing.
